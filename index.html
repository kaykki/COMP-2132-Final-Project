Dice Game

How To Play :

  • Click "Roll Dice" to start
  • To advance to the next round, click the "Roll Dice" button again
  • After three rounds, the final score is calculate, and it will show the result
  • Click "Play Again" to play again

Rules :

  • Each round, both you and your oponent will roll a pair of dice
  • The sum of your dice is your score for that round
  • If anyone rolls a "1," then your points for that round is "0"
  • If anyone rolls a double (ex. 4 and 4), then your score that round is doubled (ex. score = 16)
  • The player with the most accumulated score wins
